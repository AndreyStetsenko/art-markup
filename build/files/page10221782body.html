Alina Duhart
Alina was born in 1969 in Samara. Her creative biography began in early childhood, when the young artist felt her ability to reflect the world around her on paper and in modeling. Painting and sculpture have become an integral part of life and have formed a special attentive view of the beauty of reality. Alina Venison studied at various studios and artists, while retaining their individuality . Several years of studying at the art school and Academy seemed boring to her and the artist chose her own path . Studying the spiritual content in life, she turns to iconography and for 15 years has been painting icons that adorn many famous churches in Russia and Ukraine, are in private collections. Impressionism and realism are intertwined in images of creative search for deep spiritual beauty . An artist with a wealth of experience, working in different styles and directions, finds for each picture its own special way of expression. Smooth classical painting with a brush for a restrained surface observation of nature and Vice versa textured three-dimensional stroke with a palette knife for masculine images or bright combinations of color and form.
Personal exhibitions in Russia and abroad always arouse great interest and many paintings by Alina Olenina (Kuznetsova) are in private collections in Japan, the United States , Spain and other countries.
The artist shares her admiration and desire to create with her students-people who want to get involved in painting. Numerous followers of the artist get the necessary experience in her master classes and paint their own paintings. Alina Olenina lives and works in Moscow, being in constant creative movement.

Ruslan Magomedov
An artist who works in mixed media, mostly modern calligraphy. In the past, I was engaged in graphic and motion design - this has largely influenced the style and formed a love for digital images and modern technologies. In his Paintings, he often works with optical illusions and three-dimensional geometry. Customizing clothing and creating music is also a major part of the artist's work.
He started drawing graffiti in 2000 . in 2006, he became interested in graphic design and spent all his working and free time in graphic programs , creating design of printing products and design of night events in various clubs of the city, prints on clothes , information animation. Later, he started shooting videos and music videos . In 2015, wanting to move away from digital visualization, I decided to go back to what I started with - rethink graffiti and learn new techniques of creative expression - one of these techniques was calligraphy. After leaving to live in the mountains of Dagestan, he practiced calligraphy for 5 months . when he returned to St. Petersburg, he began to apply new knowledge on walls, canvases, cars and clothes . In 2017, he moved to Moscow where he currently writes paintings and music, works on models of author's clothing, and makes animated videos.
